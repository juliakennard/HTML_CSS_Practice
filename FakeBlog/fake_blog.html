Get Grafting: A Love Island Blog


Love Island is a UK-based reality tv dating show where men and women live together in a villa and must couple up to stay safe from elimination. As new men and women are brought into the villa, contestants must choose whether to stay loyal in their current couple or, as the show has coined, "get grafting" and find a new mate. Contestants are eliminated every week and the last couple standing wins a cash prize.

The game play mixed with drama and British lingo makes for a massively entertaining show to watch (especially when there's nothing else to do during quarantine).

Popular Love Island Lingo

  • Get grafting: Put in work to get someone to like you
  • Do bits: Get intimate
  • Get pied: Get dumped
  • Get mugged off: Be disrespected or played
